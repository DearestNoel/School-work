Company History
  • Nekorobi is one of Tokyo's oldest cat cafes, Nekorobi is a friendly, relaxed spot to hang out, although it's a lot better when it's not crowded, as there are only eleven resident cats to go around. You'll find the usual collection of cat toys, climbing trees, hideaway spots and cardboard boxes, along with low-rise, cat-level seats around the periphery of the room
  • Cats flower garden The world's first cat café, "Cat Flower Garden", opened its doors in Taipei, Taiwan, in 1998.you never feel like the cats are merely there for your entertainment — this is their home, and you are the guest. There are plenty of beds and soft spots for the cats to sleep in, and there are two cat-only compartments for them to escape to when they no longer want contact with humans. If you choose to, you can buy them treats at the front desk.
  • Catnip Cat Cafe Catnip Cat Cafe has 2,000 square feet of space for our adoptable shelter cats to roam freely 24/7. This setup provides the kitties with the opportunity to really get comfortable and show off their sparkling personalities to you. There is plenty of space for these lovable felines to live in harmony and even enjoy some companionship from the humans who visit Catnip.
